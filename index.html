About Us

Welcome to PRS Hospital, a leading healthcare institution dedicated to providing exceptional care and innovative medical services to our patients.
Our hospital has been serving the community for a long time, and we are committed to providing high-quality, patient-centered care to every individual who walks through our doors.

Our mission is to provide compassionate, comprehensive care to all patients, regardless of their age, background, or medical condition. We believe that every patient deserves the highest level of care, and we are dedicated to delivering that care in a welcoming, respectful, and supportive environment.

At PRS Hospital, we offer a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, inpatient care, diagnostic services, surgery, maternity care, mental health services, rehabilitation services, palliative and hospice care, and more. Our team of highly skilled healthcare providers includes doctors, nurses, technicians, and other medical professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible care to our patients.

We are also committed to innovation and staying at the forefront of the latest advances in medical technology and treatment. We are constantly investing in new equipment, training our staff, and exploring new approaches to care that can help us provide better outcomes and a higher quality of life for our patients. Thank you for considering PRS Hospital for your healthcare needs. We are honored to serve our community and are committed to providing the best possible care to every patient who walks through our doors.

Services

At PRS Hospital, we offer a comprehensive range of medical services to meet the healthcare needs of our community. Our team of skilled physicians, nurses, and other healthcare professionals are committed to providing compassionate, high-quality care in a welcoming and supportive environment..

Emergency Care

Hospital is equipped to handle medical emergencies 24/7, providing critical care for patients who require immediate attention for serious injuries or illnesses.

Inpatient Care

Hospital provides inpatient care for patients who require a hospital stay, such as those who are recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or receiving treatment for a serious illness

Diagnostic Services

Hospital offers a range of diagnostic services, including imaging tests like X-rays and MRIs, laboratory tests, and other tests that help diagnose medical conditions.>

Surgery

Hospital offers surgical services, including both traditional and minimally invasive surgeries, to treat a range of medical conditions.

Rehabilitation Services

Hospital provides rehabilitation services to help patients recover from injuries or illnesses, including physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy.

Transplant Services

Hospital offers specialized transplant services, including organ transplantation and other types of transplant procedures.
